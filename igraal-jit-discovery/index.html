🔴 The User Problem ✓ Research

"I want to save money, but I don't want to work for it."

iGraal's search is merchant-name only — you can search "Nike" but not "running shoes under €100." No cashback platform in the market does product-level search. Users with category intent (not brand intent) hit a wall everywhere. Meanwhile, trust issues at withdrawal create a second problem: even when users find deals, they're not sure they'll actually get paid.

🟡 The Business Problem ✓ Research

High-value offers go unseen because discovery is merchant-indexed, not need-indexed.

A user searching "shoes" won't find Zalando's 8% cashback unless they already know Zalando is a partner. No competitor solves this either — it's a category-wide structural gap. 🟢 The Opportunity ✓ Research

Move from merchant discovery → intent discovery. Be the first European cashback platform with AI-native deal finding.

Amazon Rufus proves conversational commerce works (250M users, 60% higher purchase completion) — but has no cashback layer. Ibotta proves semantic search works for deals (+15% offer unlocks) — but is US-only and grocery-centric. Nobody has combined AI discovery + cashback in Europe. iGraal can be first.

02 — Prerequisite

Fix Trust Before Shipping AI

Research shows trust is the #1 problem across the entire cashback category. No AI feature will matter if users don't believe they'll get paid. These must be addressed before or alongside the Smart Discovery launch.

⚠️ Critical finding: iGraal users on Trustpilot report aggressive document-gating at withdrawal — passport, invoices, proof of address demanded for €20 payouts. Accounts suspended mid-verification. Users describe the process as "humiliating." This is the #1 reason users churn from cashback platforms.

🔐 Progressive KYC

Verify identity at signup — not at withdrawal. Small cashbacks (< €20) without docs; larger amounts with progressive verification. Never surprise a user at the moment they want their money.

✓ Trustpilot data

✅ Real-Time Tracking Confirmation

"Your cashback is being tracked ✓" within seconds of purchase. Eliminate the anxiety gap between buying and seeing the cashback appear. This is the single biggest UX improvement across the category.

✓ Cross-platform complaint

⏱️ Payout Timeline UI

"Your €12.50 becomes available on May 15 — 47 days." A countdown transforms frustration into expectation. Plus: promote iGraal's 24-hour gift card cashback as the instant alternative.

✓ Rakuten 120-day pain point

iGraal's secret weapon: 24-hour gift card cashback. While Rakuten makes users wait up to 120 days, iGraal can deliver within 24 hours via gift cards. This should be a first-class feature in every mode — not hidden in a submenu. It's the most powerful differentiator iGraal has.

04 — User Pain Points

What Users Actually Complain About

Synthesised from Trustpilot, Reddit (r/Rakuten, r/arnaques, r/vosfinances, r/SavingMoney), Google Play reviews, and app store listings across all major cashback platforms.

1. Cashback Doesn't Track
📍 All platforms — most acute at Honey, Rakuten, ShopBack, iGraal
"I've never had all offers track at once without emailing them with screenshots."
Design response: Real-time "✓ Tracked" confirmation within seconds. Eliminate the anxiety gap.
2. Document-Gating at Withdrawal
📍 iGraal (most acute), Honey, ShopBack
"Why demand my passport to release €20 when you didn't need it to take commission?"
Design response: Progressive KYC at signup. Never surprise users at payout time.
3. Endless Payout Waits
📍 Rakuten (120 days), TopCashback, all others
"Cashback confirmed then reverted to pending right before quarterly payout."
Design response: Countdown UI + promote 24h gift card cashback as instant alternative.
4. Coupons That Don't Work
📍 Honey (post-scandal), Capital One Shopping
"Half the time it doesn't find codes that I know exist."
Design response: Show the process: "We tested 4 codes. This one saved you 15%." Transparency builds trust.
5. Forgetting to Activate
📍 Rakuten, TopCashback, iGraal (web)
"Completed purchase then realised I forgot to click through the portal."
Design response: Proactive push notifications before checkout. Browse mode feeds intent data to alerts.
6. Surprise Account Suspensions
📍 Honey, iGraal, ShopBack
"Accused of abuse for manually claiming cashback that didn't auto-track."
Design response: Clear, specific suspension notices. Never punish users for using the product as intended.

The irony across the category: platforms' own tracking failures cause users to claim manually → platforms then suspend them for overclaiming. The system punishes users for compensating for its own failures. iGraal can break this cycle by fixing tracking first.

06 — Interaction Modes

Three Paths, One Destination

Every mode leads to the same JIT-composed answer screen. The entry differs, the output is unified. Users don't choose a mode — the app detects it from their behavior. All three modes should share context — an Ask about sportswear should influence the Browse feed for the next week.

🗣️
Ask Mode
Conversational — natural language → AI-composed answer
  1. Entry: User taps the Ask prompt or speaks. "Best deal on headphones under €80"
  2. Understanding: Semantic intent parsing (not keyword matching). Reference: Ibotta's vector search model.
  3. JIT Assembly: Cashback merchants + promo codes + savings comparison + 24h gift card option — all composed per query.
  4. Refinement: "What about wireless?" → screen updates in place. Context preserved. Reference: Rufus' conversation memory.
  5. Action: One-tap cashback activation → merchant redirect with tracking confirmation.
Validated: Rufus 60% purchase lift △ Needs iGraal-specific testing
🔍
Search Mode
Structured — keyword → smart results → JIT answer
  1. Entry: User types keyword: "Nike" or "electronics" or "travel deals." Today this returns only merchant pages.
  2. Smart categorization: Results auto-grouped: merchants by cashback %, active codes, price comparison, trending deals — not a flat merchant list.
  3. Rate transparency: Cashback % visible in results (steal from TopCashback). Cross-retailer comparison (steal from Capital One Shopping).
  4. AI nudge: "Looking for Nike? Zalando has 8% cashback + free shipping code — saves you €12 more than buying direct." Helpful, not intrusive.
  5. Action: Quick-tap cashback activation + "Want it in 24 hours? Buy a gift card here."
Validated: Ibotta +15% unlocks, −73% zero results Recommended first launch
🎁
Browse Mode
Proactive — curated feed → personalized deal surfaces
  1. Entry: User opens app with no intent. Lands on personalised feed (not category grid).
  2. Smart feed: Composed from: purchase history, browsing within app, cashback rate spikes, seasonal context, urgency (expiring deals).
  3. Gamification: Adapted from ShopBack's quest system — challenges based on user's actual shopping patterns, not merchant payments. "Shop 3 fashion brands this week → earn €5 bonus."
  4. Countdown timers: On boosted deals (steal from ShopBack). Creates urgency without manipulation. Monthly recurring events train habitual engagement.
  5. Action: Each feed card expands to full JIT deal surface. Swipe to save, tap to shop.
Gap: No competitor has a personalised feed △ Quest design needs user testing

Launch order recommendation: Search Mode first (highest user volume, lowest risk, Ibotta-validated metrics). Then Ask Mode (higher value, needs AI investment, test with power users first). Then Browse Mode (highest engagement potential, needs personalisation engine). Incremental rollout, not big bang.

🔍 Search Mode — The Structural Leap

Current iGraal: merchant-name-only → "shoes" returns nothing

Smart Discovery: intent-indexed → "shoes" returns:

  • 🏷️ Best Cashback — Zalando 8%, Nike 5%, ASOS 6% (rate transparency from TopCashback)
  • 🎟️ Active Codes — 3 verified, success rates shown (transparency counter to Honey scandal)
  • 📊 Cross-Retailer Comparison — "Zalando saves you €12 more" (steal from Capital One Shopping)
  • 🎁 24h Gift Card Option — "Want instant cashback? Buy a Zalando gift card"
  • 🔔 Price Alert — "Nike Air Max dropped 15% at 2 stores"

Target metric: Ibotta's −73% zero-result searches. The search box becomes a deal engine.

🎁 Browse Mode — Feed Composition Logic

Feed algorithm (research-informed):

  • ⏰ Urgency — expiring deals + countdown timers (steal from ShopBack)
  • 📈 Cashback Spikes — merchants who just increased rates
  • 👤 Personal — purchase history within iGraal (strongest signal)
  • 🎯 Quests — adapted from ShopBack: user-pattern-based, not merchant-funded (avoid manipulation complaint)
  • 🗓️ Recurring Events — monthly travel sale, seasonal campaigns (steal from ShopBack's fixed calendar)
  • 🔔 Proactive Nudge — "You bought from Fnac last week — 10% cashback is back"

Key principle: sponsored deals must be clearly labeled. Users know when they're being sold to — transparency wins.

08 — JIT Component Engine

How Screens Are Assembled

The JIT engine selects from a palette of composable components based on intent analysis. No two answer screens look the same — they're built from the same blocks, assembled per query. Components are influenced by competitive research.

💰 Cashback Merchant Card
Merchant logo, cashback %, status, "Shop Now" CTA + rate transparency (cashback % in results, steal from TopCashback).
data: merchant, rate, status, logo, deeplink, rateHistory
🎟️ Promo Code Card (Transparent)
"We tested 4 codes. This one saved 15%." Show the process — counter to Honey's code suppression scandal. Success rate badge, expiry, conditions.
data: code, merchant, expiry, successRate, testedCount
📊 Cross-Retailer Comparison
Same product/category across 2-3 merchants: price + cashback + codes = total savings. Steal from Capital One Shopping's comparison UX.
data: merchants[], rates[], prices[], totalSavings[]
🧮 Stacked Savings Calculator
Power users: cashback + promo code + gift card cashback = total. Interactive amount input. Makes iGraal's stacking capability visible.
data: cashback, promoValue, giftCardBonus, total
🎁 24h Gift Card Card
iGraal's killer feature, promoted. "Want your cashback in 24 hours? Buy a [Merchant] gift card — earn [X]% instantly." First-class, not hidden.
data: merchant, giftCardRate, deliveryTime, available
⏰ Countdown Banner
Steal from ShopBack: countdown for expiring/boosted deals. "This rate ends in 4h 23m." Urgency without manipulation — only for real expirations.
data: expiryTimestamp, dealTitle, normalRate, boostedRate
📈 Rate Trend Graph
Cashback rate history: "Rate is at 12-month high" or "Usually higher in December." Empowers Savvy Maximizers to time purchases.
data: merchant, rateHistory[], currentRate, trend, forecast
✅ Tracking Confirmation
Post-purchase: "Your cashback is being tracked ✓" within seconds. The most requested feature across every cashback platform. Trust signal #1.
data: orderId, merchant, amount, status, estimatedPayout
🏆 Quest Card
Adapted from ShopBack: user-pattern-based challenges. "Shop 2 fashion brands → earn €3 bonus." Progress bar + countdown. Must feel achievable, not manipulative.
data: questTitle, progress, reward, expiry, merchantPool
🔔 Smart Alert Card
No results → "Set alert for Nike cashback above 8%." Turns zero-result into retention. Also: "Activate cashback before you shop" push notification.
data: category, threshold, channel, linkedPurchaseHistory

Assembly logic: Intent Engine scores each component (0-1) based on query + user history + context. Components above 0.6 threshold included. The 24h Gift Card Card should appear in every result set where available — it's the trust differentiator. Output is a VStack — familiar, scrollable, native-feeling.

The Stuff That Kills You

Updated with real failure patterns from across the category. Each edge case is now informed by actual user complaints.

12 — Competitive Landscape

What to Steal, What to Exploit

Based on deep competitive teardown: app reviews, user forums, published case studies, and market analysis. Each competitor has one thing worth stealing and one fatal weakness to exploit.

🍯 Honey (PayPal) Trust destroyed

Lost 8M users. Cookie-stuffing scandal. Code suppression for merchant partners. Multiple class actions. Google forced Chrome policy changes. Down from 20M → 12M users.

✅ Steal

Checkout interception (passive, zero-friction). Droplist price monitoring.

🎯 Exploit

Position iGraal as the anti-Honey: "We never swap your codes, never take commission without saving you money, never sell your data."

🛍️ Rakuten Payout friction

Category leader. 3,500+ stores. Excellent tracking. "Missing Cash Back" instant credit on disputes. But: quarterly-only payouts — up to 120-day wait.

✅ Steal

"Missing Cash Back" instant credit mechanism. Amex Points as alternative payout.

🎯 Exploit

120-day wait vs. iGraal 24-hour gift card. Instant gratification is the weapon.

🛒 ShopBack Best engagement

50M+ users. Best gamification: quest system, countdown timers, ShopBack Play games. ShopBack GO for in-store. $5 minimum withdrawal. Monthly recurring promotions.

✅ Steal

Quest system, countdown timers, fixed monthly events, $5 min threshold, shipping rebates.

🎯 Exploit

Tracking reliability is their Achilles heel. iGraal's 24h gift card = certainty vs. their uncertain tracking.

🧪 Ibotta AI benchmark

Public company (NYSE: IBTA). Most advanced AI: vector search +15% unlocks, −73% zero results. ML personalisation. 200M+ consumer reach via IPN. But US-only, grocery-centric.

✅ Steal

Vector search model, loyalty card linking (zero friction), "Any Item" category offers.

🎯 Exploit

US-only + grocery-only. iGraal can apply the same AI playbook to a broader European surface area.

🤖 ChatGPT Shopping Platform threat

Conversational product discovery + Instant Checkout (Sept 2025). Agentic Commerce Protocol with Stripe. Target partnership (Nov 2025). Ad-free results. No cashback layer.

✅ Steal

Product card format, conversational refinement UX, zero-context-switching.

🎯 Exploit

No cashback. "ChatGPT Shopping + cashback baked in" = iGraal's Ask mode positioning.

🔍 Amazon Rufus Platform threat

250M users, +60% purchase completion. Conversation memory + agentic cart building. Multimodal (text, voice, image). Limited to Amazon catalogue.

✅ Steal

Purchase memory ("reorder what I bought last month"), ever-present chat (not siloed), voice input.

🎯 Exploit

Amazon-only. iGraal spans 4,000+ merchants. Cross-retailer discovery is the differentiator.

The gap nobody owns: Conversational AI discovery + cashback + cross-retailer comparison + instant payout — in Europe. ChatGPT and Rufus have the AI but no cashback. Rakuten and ShopBack have the cashback but no AI. Ibotta has both but only in US grocery. iGraal can be the first to combine all four in Europe. The window is open — but ChatGPT's Agentic Commerce Protocol is coming.
